About Goa

Goa is a state on the southwestern coast of India known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage.

Main Attractions

  • Beaches like Baga and Calangute
  • Old Goa churches
  • Dudhsagar Waterfalls
  • Spice plantations

Culture

Goa's culture is a blend of Portuguese and Indian traditions, reflected in its festivals, food, and architecture.
